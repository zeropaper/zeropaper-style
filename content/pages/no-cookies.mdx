# No Cookies

**TL;DR: don't sue me for visiting this web site or install it for offline usage**

This web site doesn't use cookies and has no interests in tracking you. 
However you could not even access it if you had no connexions to Internet, if it wasn't hosted on a server (its provider is [Infomaniak](https://www.infomaniak.com/en/legal/confidentiality-policy)) or you were not using a browser.  
These - third-party - services may collect information about your connection and I have no control over that.

If this website saves something in your browser, you will be asked before it does.
